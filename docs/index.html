Biography

I have recently obtained my PhD degree from NeuroPoly Lab at Polytechnique Montreal (supervisor Dr. Nikola Stikov).

My research focuses on bringing quantitative MRI (qMRI) applications under one umbrella through data standardization, vendor-neutral pulse sequence development, fully transparent & reproducible workflows and community building.

My motivation for open science is born from a combination of open-source development (BrainHack, MRathon, OpenMR) and science communication (MRM Highlights, OHBM Blog, ISMRM’s MRPulse).
